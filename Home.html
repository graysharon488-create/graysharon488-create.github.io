Biology Classroom

Welcome to the lab! This site hopes to provide free, easy to access labs, to empower student's learning. New Modules hopefully coming soon! The current modules aims to be at a level in which the simulation labs could instruct students in advanced placement. With further updates, plans are being made to allow for students of different grades and ages to experience fun learning labs, without all the hassle and cost of setting up a physical one. This website can also be used to help students study or just as extra practice problems. Happy learning!
